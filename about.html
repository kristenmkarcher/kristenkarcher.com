    • A little about me.

    Technology and art are two things that have surrounded me throughout my life. While I grew up around the latest technology, I was always more fascinated with art. Pen to paper, paint to canvas; my childhood focused on drawing, painting, and lettering. After a few years of self discovery, I decided to combine these two constants in my life and pursue web design. I love bright and bold designs, but I excel at translating any idea into reality.

    While not working, I can be found lifting weights, enjoying the outdoors, working with various artistic mediums, and going for long walks with my three dogs. I am always looking to meet and collaborate with others, and continue growing my skills. If you like what you see, feel free to reach out!
